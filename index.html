Complete GTA San Andreas Missions List

(With Rewards & Tips)

The GTA San Andreas missions list is one of the most extensive in the Grand Theft Auto series. With over 100 story missions, players follow Carl "CJ" Johnson as he rises through the criminal underworld across Los Santos, San Fierro, and Las Venturas. Each mission pushes the storyline forward while unlocking new locations, abilities, properties, and income opportunities.

01

Los Santos Missions

The game begins in Los Santos, where CJ reconnects with the Grove Street Families. Early missions like "Big Smoke," "Ryder," and "Tagging Up Turf" introduce core mechanics including shooting, driving, and territory control. As the story progresses, players take on high-stakes assignments like "Reuniting the Families" and "The Green Sabre."

Rewards

  • Unlocking new weapons
  • Respect and stamina boosts
  • Access to new city areas
Pro Tip

Focus on improving weapon skills early. Higher weapon proficiency improves accuracy and reduces recoil during intense firefights.

02

San Fierro Missions

After major events in Los Santos, CJ moves to San Fierro. Missions such as "Wear Flowers in Your Hair," "555 We Tip," and "Photo Opportunity" expand gameplay with stealth, photography, and infiltration elements. This stage also introduces asset-building through businesses like the garage in Doherty.

Rewards

  • New safehouses
  • Business income
  • Access to Zero's missions
Pro Tip

Invest time in side activities to increase stamina and driving skills before tackling more difficult operations in this region.

03

Las Venturas Missions

Las Venturas focuses heavily on casino operations and large-scale heists. Missions like "Fender Ketchup," "Explosive Situation," and "Breaking the Bank at Caligula's" require strategic planning and preparation.

Rewards

  • Major cash payouts
  • Unlocking high-end vehicles
  • Stronger gang influence
Pro Tip

Complete preparation missions carefully before attempting the casino heist. Stock up on armor and weapons to avoid mission failure late in the sequence.

04

Final Los Santos Return

CJ eventually returns to Los Santos for climactic missions such as "Home Coming" and "End of the Line." These final chapters wrap up long-running rivalries and determine the fate of Grove Street.

Rewards

  • Massive respect boost
  • Final storyline completion
  • Full map control
Pro Tip

Keep your health, armor, and ammunition maxed before the final mission. The last sequence includes multiple waves of enemies.

How to Complete All Missions Efficiently

Build Stamina

Increase stamina through cycling and running.

Upgrade Skills

Upgrade weapon skills regularly.

Purchase Properties

Purchase properties to save progress frequently.

Multiple Saves

Keep multiple save files before difficult missions.
